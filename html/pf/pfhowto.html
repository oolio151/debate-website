The Structure

Public Forum is composed of many speeches and crossfires. The general layout for a round is as below, and we will go more in depth on this throughout the page. You can click on the different stages to see more info on them.

  • First Case - 4 min
  • Second Case - 4 min
  • First Crossfire - 3 min
  • First Rebuttal - 4 min
  • Second Rebuttal - 4 min
  • Second Crossfire - 3 min
  • First Summary - 3 min
  • Second Summary - 3 min
  • Grand Crossfire - 3 min
  • First Final Focus - 2 min
  • Second Final Focus - 2 min

Each team also gets 3 minutes of prep time which they can use freely between speeches.
